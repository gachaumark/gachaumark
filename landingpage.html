Our Vision

"They call us the informal sector, but we are the backbone of Kenya!"

Fellow drivers, conductors, and operators: stand tall today. When our engines stop, the entire country feels the pulse of our absenc e. We do not strike out of choice; we strike out of survival.

Every day, we face skyrocketing fuel, broken roads, and relentless harassment. Yet, we are the ones who move the workforce, the students, and the economy. We deserve respect, not extortion!

Our unity is our ultimate horsepower. Do not let them divide us or silence our demands. Keep the keys out of the ign ition, stay peaceful, and stand firm. If we do not fight for our livelihood today, we will have nothing left to drive tomorrow.

Let us stand together until they listen!
